# 1. AI Fluency

AI Fluency means the ability to effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely work with AI systems.

AI Fluency helps people:
- Work better with AI tools
- Improve productivity
- Think critically about AI outputs
- Use AI responsibly

---

# Core Principles of AI Fluency

- Effective
- Efficient
- Ethical
- Safe

---

# The 4 D's of AI Fluency

## 1. Delegation

Understanding:
- What humans should do
- What AI should do
- What humans and AI should do together

### Key Concepts

### Problem Awareness
Clearly understanding goals before using AI.

### Platform Awareness
Knowing the strengths and limitations of AI systems.

### Task Delegation
Distributing work between humans and AI effectively.

---

## 2. Description

Having productive conversations with AI systems.

### Product Description
Clearly defining:
- Output
- Format
- Audience
- Style

### Process Description
Giving step-by-step instructions to AI.

### Performance Description
Defining AI behavior:
- concise
- detailed
- supportive
- analytical

---

## 3. Discernment

Critically evaluating AI outputs and behavior.

### Product Discernment
Checking:
- quality
- accuracy
- usefulness

### Process Discernment
Evaluating:
- logic
- reasoning
- consistency

### Performance Discernment
Checking whether AI communication style is suitable.

---

## 4. Diligence

Taking responsibility while using AI.

### Transparency Diligence
Being honest about AI usage.

### Deployment Diligence
Verifying outputs before sharing.

### Creation Diligence
Choosing appropriate AI systems responsibly.
